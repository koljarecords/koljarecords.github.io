Label Notes

Music. Freedom. Forever.

Kölja Records was started in 2026 in São Paulo by a producer who got tired of waiting for permission. The catalog is intentionally personal: aliases, side projects, demos, singles and full-volume experiments kept under one roof.

Every release is hand-tagged, numbered and kept in one editable source of truth. When the catalog changes, the site and the offline editor change together.
